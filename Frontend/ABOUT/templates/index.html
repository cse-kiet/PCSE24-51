Get active, wherever you want

Posture Guard is an innovative system that uses computer vision and artificial intelligence (AI) to provide safe and efficient exercise monitoring in real-time. The system uses a camera to capture the user's movements and analyze them based on factors such as joint angles and range of motion. Posture Guard provides accurate and objective measurements of exercise performance, which can be used to optimize training and prevent injury.

INTRODUCTION

Posture Guard a product that aims to lead the users to a better future by working on smaller steps that we as humans take everyday.Almost everyone knows about simple exercises and workouts like bicep curl, but only a few out of those know the correct form and are able to maintain it.That's why we are working on Posture Guard

Problem Statement

According to a study in 2014, knee injuries account for the highest (23%) among the common gym-related injuries, followed by injuries to the ankle and foot (16%), shoulder (14%), lower back (12%), neck (11%) and prolapsed or slipped disc (2%). The remaining 22% comprise injuries to the elbow, muscle strains, pulls and tears, and tendon ruptures.

Safety

The first concern of everyone going to the gym is saftey during the workout. The risks of injury for first timers and ego lifters are exponentially higher since their body is not adapted to the weights and stress due to bad form and irregular stance

Efficiency

Now if you are sustaining such risks and going to gym daily, you would expect to see results even if a its a small one. But many people loose out on the maximum benefit since they cannot afford a personal trainer and rely on inefficient and irregular form causing little to no results.

OBJECTIVE

This project is our work towards the objective of reducing and possibly prevention of injuries. The project aims to do this by keeping a check on the exercise routine by the user and alerting the user whenever the issue can become hazardous to their health.

The broad objective of this project is to enable the people to work out/exercise anywhere be it professional gym or at home, without worrying about wasting their efforts or harming themselves.

CONNECTED WITH TELEGRAM

Posture Guard provides personalized workout plans and tracks users' fitness progress. It uses machine learning algorithms to analyze data on the user's fitness level, physical activity, and health status to create customized workout plans and nutrition recommendations.

Users can connect to Posture Guard through the Telegram bot. By sending messages to the Posture Guard bot on Telegram, users can receive personalized workout plans and track their fitness progress without the need for a dedicated app or personal trainer.

COMPARISON

One of the solutions that was compared with Posture Guard was Fitbit, a popular wearable fitness tracker. While Fitbit offers basic activity tracking and heart rate monitoring, it does not provide personalized workout plans or real-time feedback on form or technique. In contrast, Posture Guard offers advanced safety features, such as form analysis and personalized feedback, to help users reduce the risk of injury and optimize their workouts.

COMPARISON

Another solution that was compared with Posture Guard was MyFitnessPal, a mobile application that allows users to track their food intake and exercise. While MyFitnessPal offers some basic workout tracking features, it does not provide personalized workout plans or advanced safety features.

COMPARISON

Finally, another solution that was compared with Posture Guard was Mirror, an AI-powered workout assistant that uses computer vision to provide real-time feedback on form and technique. While Mirror offers real-time feedback, it does not provide personalized workout plans or advanced safety features. Posture Guard, on the other hand, offers both personalized workout plans and advanced safety features, as well as real-time feedback on form and technique.
